# Privacy Policy

**shyield.finance**  
Effective Date: November 28, 2025  
Last Updated: November 28, 2025

Shield Finance ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website https://shyield.finance (the "Site"), use our decentralized application (dApp), connect your wallet, or otherwise interact with our services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this privacy policy carefully. By accessing or using the Services, you acknowledge that you have read, understood, and agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you do not agree, please do not access or use the Services.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **Wallet Address**: When you connect your cryptocurrency wallet (via Xaman, MetaMask, WalletConnect, or other supported providers)
- **Transaction Signatures**: Messages and transactions you approve through your wallet
- **Communications**: Any information you voluntarily submit (e.g., email if you contact support)

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

**On-Chain Data (Public by Design)**
- Wallet addresses
- Transaction history
- Token balances
- Smart contract interactions
- Staking positions and rewards

::: warning On-Chain Data Is Public
All blockchain transactions are permanently recorded on public ledgers (Flare Network, XRP Ledger). This data cannot be deleted or modified.
:::

**Off-Chain Analytics (Privacy-Respecting)**
- Anonymized IP address (truncated, not stored)
- Browser type and version
- Approximate geographic location (country-level only)
- Pages visited and interaction patterns
- Device type (mobile/desktop)

We use **Plausible Analytics**, a privacy-focused analytics platform that:
- Does not use cookies
- Does not collect personal data
- Does not track users across websites
- Is fully GDPR compliant

### 1.3 Cookies

We use only **strictly necessary cookies** required for the basic functionality of our Services:
- Session management
- Wallet connection state
- User preferences (theme, language)

We do **not** use:
- Tracking cookies
- Advertising cookies
- Third-party marketing cookies

See our [Cookie Policy](/legal/cookie-policy) for more details.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

- **Provide Services**: Process transactions, display your positions, show staking rewards
- **Improve the Platform**: Debug issues, optimize performance, enhance user experience
- **Security**: Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, or security threats

::: tip We Do Not Sell Your Data
We will never sell, rent, or trade your personal information to third parties for marketing purposes.

## 6. Your Rights (GDPR/CCPA)

You have the right to:

- **Access**: Request a copy of data we hold about you
- **Rectification**: Correct inaccurate personal data
- **Erasure**: Request deletion of off-chain personal data
- **Portability**: Receive your data in a portable format
- **Opt-Out**: Disable analytics tracking at any time
- **Withdraw Consent**: Stop receiving communications

::: warning Blockchain Limitation
On-chain data (transactions, balances, contract interactions) **cannot be deleted** due to the immutable nature of blockchain technology.
:::
