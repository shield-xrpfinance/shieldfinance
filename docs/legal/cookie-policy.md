# Cookie Policy

**shyield.finance**  
Effective Date: November 28, 2025  
Last Updated: November 28, 2025

This Cookie Policy explains how Shield Finance ("we," "our," or "us") uses cookies and similar technologies when you visit our website at https://shyield.finance (the "Site") or use our decentralized application (the "Services").

---

## 1. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile phone) when you visit websites. They help websites remember your preferences and improve your experience.

---

## 2. Our Cookie Philosophy

::: tip Privacy-First Approach
Shield Finance is committed to user privacy. We use a **minimal cookie approach**:

- **No tracking cookies**
- **No advertising cookies**
- **No third-party marketing cookies**
- **No cross-site tracking**
:::

We only use cookies that are strictly necessary for the basic functionality of our Services.

---

## 3. Types of Cookies We Use

### 3.1 Strictly Necessary Cookies

These cookies are essential for the Site to function properly. They cannot be disabled.

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintains your session state | Session |
| `wallet_connected` | Remembers if wallet is connected | Session |
| `theme` | Stores your theme preference (light/dark) | 1 year |
| `language` | Stores your language preference | 1 year |
| `consent` | Records your cookie consent choice | 1 year |

### 3.2 Functional Cookies

These cookies enable enhanced functionality:

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `preferred_wallet` | Remembers your preferred wallet provider | 30 days |
| `slippage_tolerance` | Stores your swap slippage setting | 30 days |
| `display_currency` | Remembers your display currency (USD, EUR, etc.) | 30 days |

## 5. Analytics: Plausible

Instead of cookie-based analytics, we use **Plausible Analytics**, a privacy-focused analytics platform that:

- Does **not** use cookies
- Does **not** collect personal data
- Does **not** track users across websites
- Is fully GDPR, CCPA, and PECR compliant
- Stores data in the EU

Plausible provides us with:
- Page view counts
- Referral sources
- Country-level location (not precise)
- Device type (mobile/desktop)
- Browser type

All data is anonymous and aggregated. No individual user can be identified.

## 7. Local Storage and Session Storage

In addition to cookies, we use browser storage for:

| Storage Type | Purpose |
|--------------|---------|
| `localStorage` | Theme preference, wallet connection state |
| `sessionStorage` | Temporary transaction data, form state |

This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

---

## 8. How to Manage Cookies

### 8.1 Browser Settings

You can control cookies through your browser settings:

- **Chrome**: Settings → Privacy and Security → Cookies
- **Firefox**: Options → Privacy & Security → Cookies
- **Safari**: Preferences → Privacy → Cookies
- **Edge**: Settings → Privacy → Cookies

### 8.2 Opt-Out Links

- [Plausible Opt-Out](https://plausible.io/privacy) (not needed as Plausible doesn't track)
- [Your Online Choices](https://www.youronlinechoices.eu/)
- [Network Advertising Initiative](https://optout.networkadvertising.org/)

::: warning Functionality Impact
Blocking strictly necessary cookies may prevent some features from working correctly, such as:
- Wallet connection persistence
- Theme preferences
- Session management
:::

## 9. Do Not Track (DNT)

We honor Do Not Track browser signals. When DNT is enabled, we:
- Disable any optional analytics
- Minimize data collection
- Do not track user behavior

## 10. Cookie Consent

On your first visit, we display a cookie consent banner. You can:

- **Accept All**: Enable all cookie categories

- **Customize**: Choose specific cookie categories

You can change your preferences at any time by:
- Clicking the cookie settings icon in the footer
- Clearing your browser cookies
- Contacting us at privacy@shyield.finance
